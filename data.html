2. What are its main predators? The main predators to stag beetles are magpies, they leave the beetle in a characteristic way, just head, thorax and wing cases, with some or all of the legs. The beetles can survive like this for up to several days. Many people find beetles like this and are very distressed about it and curious to know why it has happened.
